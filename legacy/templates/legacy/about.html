About "Byte of Legacy"

Byte of Legacy is more than a digital wall — it’s a living mosaic of stories, memories, and dreams. Each byte you claim is a meaningful mark on our collective startup journey.

Our Vision

We’re building a unique platform where individuals, friends, and supporters can eternally leave their legacy — a pixel-sized footprint on a bigger story. Unlike traditional fundraising, here you get to be part of an interactive art piece, permanently showcased for the world.

Your support fuels the growth of our startup, helping turn ideas into reality. In return, you gain not just a space on the wall, but a share in our story.

How It Works

  1. Claim your Byte: Choose your spot on the digital wall by submitting your name, message, and optional link or icon.

  2. Support our Journey: Make a manual payment via GCash or SeaBank and upload proof of your support.

  3. Be Featured: After verification, your byte is permanently displayed, part of the mosaic of our community.
