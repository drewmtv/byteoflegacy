About "Byte of Legacy"

Byte of Legacy is more than a digital wall — it’s a living mosaic of stories, memories, and dreams. Each byte you claim is a meaningful mark on our collective startup journey.

Powered by TechStart TV

Byte of Legacy is proudly built by TechStart TV, a Cebu-based grassroots media startup focused on making tech, startups, and innovation more accessible and inspiring across the Philippines.

From podcasts and educational series to community coverage, TechStart TV is committed to telling stories that matter. This wall serves as our first-ever crowdfunding campaign — helping us grow through creativity and community.

Our Vision

We’re building a space where names, memories, jokes, and dedications live forever. Unlike traditional fundraising, your support becomes a permanent part of an interactive and visual story — a digital monument powered by people.

Every slot you claim helps fund the development of TechStart TV and future storytelling tools, experiences, and platforms for the innovation ecosystem.

How It Works

  1. Claim your Byte: Pick a slot and personalize it with your name, message, and optional icon or link.

  2. Support our Journey: Send a one-time payment via GCash or SeaBank and upload proof.

  3. Be Featured: After admin verification, your byte appears live — part of our growing legacy wall.

Join Us

This is more than fundraising — it’s about being remembered, supporting local creators, and building a future together. Whether you’re a student, founder, fur parent, or believer in grassroots innovation — your byte matters.
